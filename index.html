A LITTLE BIT ABOUT MYSELF

Etah Tambe Nyenti born on Feb 27, better known by his stage name Tzy Panchak, is a Cameroonian recording artist and performer. His 2015 cover of woju “Mado” was well-received throughout Cameroon and other parts of the world. Tzy Panchak recently signed a record deal with Blunation in Cameroon, Tzy Panchak relocated to Calabar ,Nigeria at a young age. where he did his university Education and graduated with a B.SC in Computer Sciences. Tzy then returned to Cameroon where he began his music career. He released tracks like “No worry”,”Salute” Ft Chantman, and “Stay with me . Tzy Panchak has performed in shows all over Cameroon and has a massive fan base in the USA. He released his first official single titled Makaveli under new label Blu nation. Expect the unexpected from him, it is tzyyy Panchakooo you dunno.
